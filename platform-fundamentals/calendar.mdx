---
title: "Calendar"
icon: "calendar"
---

The calendar is found in the Feed tab across platform areas, including groups, projects, and profiles. Assigned tasks with set start and end dates automatically appear on the calendar.

- When viewing a group or project page, the calendar will display the events relevant to that project or group.  It provides a shared space to coordinate meetings, milestones, and events, ensuring that collaborators remain aligned throughout the work lifecycle.
- When viewing the calendar in your profile, You will see an aggregate of the events from the groups and projects you are a part of, and are able to add your own events.
