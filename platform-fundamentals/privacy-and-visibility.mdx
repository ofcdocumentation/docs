The platform gives you granular control over who can see your profile, content, and activity. This guide explains your privacy options and how to configure them.

## Visibility Levels

Most content on the platform can be set to one of three visibility levels:

| Level | Who Can See It |
| --- | --- |
| **Public** | Anyone, including people not logged in |
| **Platform Members** | Anyone with an account on the platform |
| **Connected Only** | Only your connections and group members |
| **Private** | Only you (and org admins, for org content) |

## Profile Privacy

### Your Personal Profile

Control your personal profile visibility at **Settings → Privacy → Profile**:

- **Profile visibility** – Who can view your full profile
- **Activity visibility** – Who can see your activity feed
- **Connection list** – Who can see your connections

### Your Organizational Profile

Organization admins can set the organization’s default visibility. Individual content can override the default setting.

## Content Privacy

### Setting Visibility When Creating Content

When you create a project, resource, or group, you’ll be prompted to set its visibility. You can change this at any time from the content’s settings.

### Changing Visibility of Existing Content

1. Navigate to the content you want to update
2. Click **Settings** or the **⋯** menu
3. Select **Visibility**
4. Choose the new visibility level
5. Save your changes

## Search Visibility

You can control whether your profile and content appear in search results:

- Go to **Settings → Privacy → Search**
- Toggle search visibility on or off for your profile
- Manage discoverability settings for your content

## Connection and Message Privacy

- **Who can send you connection requests** – Anyone, connections-of-connections only, or no one
- **Who can message you** – Anyone, connected users only, or no one
- **Read receipts** – Whether message senders know you’ve read their message

Configure these at **Settings → Privacy → Connections & Messages**.

## Data and Account Privacy

### Download Your Data

You can request a copy of your data at any time:

1. Go to **Settings → Privacy → Your Data**
2. Click **Download Your Data**
3. You’ll receive an email with a link to download your data archive

### Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account, contact support or go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Note that deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

<Warning>
  Account deletion permanently removes your profile, content, and connections. Make sure to download your data first.
</Warning>
