This guide gives you a bird’s-eye view of the platform and explains how to navigate between its different areas.

## Platform Overview

The platform is organized into several key areas, each serving a distinct purpose. Understanding how they fit together will help you get the most out of your experience.

## Main Navigation

### Top Navigation Bar

The top navigation bar is always visible and provides access to:

- **Home** – Your personalized activity feed
- **Search** – Find people, projects, groups, and resources
- **Notifications** – Updates on your activity and connections
- **Messages** – Direct communication with your connections
- **Profile Menu** – Access your profile, settings, and account options

### Left Sidebar

The left sidebar gives quick access to:

- **My Feed** – Content from people and organizations you follow
- **My Projects** – Projects you’re a member of
- **My Groups** – Groups you belong to
- **Saved Items** – Content you’ve bookmarked

## Finding What You Need

### Global Search

Use the search bar at the top of any page to search across all platform areas simultaneously. Filter results by type (projects, people, groups, resources) to narrow your results.

### Browse Directories

Each platform area has its own browsable directory:

- Browse all public projects at **Platform Areas → Projects**
- Browse all groups at **Platform Areas → Groups**
- Browse published resources at **Platform Areas → Resources**

### Your Feed

Your home feed shows content from people and organizations you follow, projects you’re part of, and groups you belong to.
