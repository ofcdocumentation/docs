Connections let you build your network on the platform, follow people and organizations whose work you care about, and collaborate more easily.

## Types of Connections

### Following

Follow any user or organization to see their public activity in your feed. Follows are one-directional — you don’t need their approval.

### Connected

Sending and accepting a connection request creates a mutual relationship. Connected users can message each other and see each other’s connection-only content.

### Organizational Membership

When you join an organization’s group, you’re automatically connected to that organization’s updates and announcements.

## How to Connect

### Connect with a User

1. Visit a user’s profile page
2. Click **Connect** to send a connection request
3. Once they accept, you’ll be mutually connected

### Follow an Organization

1. Visit an organization’s profile page
2. Click **Follow** to follow their updates

### Connect via Projects or Groups

When you collaborate in a shared project or group, you’ll see suggestions to connect with your collaborators.

## Managing Your Connections

View and manage all your connections from **Settings → Connections**:

- See who you’re connected with
- View pending connection requests
- Remove connections
- Manage your followers and following list

## Privacy and Connections

You control who can send you connection requests. Go to **Settings → Privacy** to:

- Allow connection requests from anyone
- Limit requests to people in your groups
- Turn off connection requests entirely

See [Privacy & Visibility](/platform-fundamentals/privacy-and-visibility) for more details.
