Your profile is your identity on the platform. This guide walks you through setting up your profile so you can connect, collaborate, and get the most out of your experience.

## Why Your Profile Matters

A complete profile helps others find and connect with you, builds trust within the community, and unlocks collaboration features across the platform.

## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Create Your Account

If you haven't already, sign up for an account. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your address.

### 2. Add Your Basic Information

Navigate to **Settings → Profile** and fill in:

- **Display Name** – How others will see you on the platform
- **Username** – Your unique handle (cannot be changed once set)
- **Bio** – A brief description of who you are and what you do
- **Location** – Optional, but helpful for local connections

### 3. Upload a Profile Photo

A profile photo makes your account more recognizable. Click the avatar placeholder on your profile page and upload an image.

<Tip>
  Use a square image at least 200×200 pixels for best results.
</Tip>

### 4. Set Your Notification Preferences

Choose how you want to be notified about activity on the platform. Go to **Settings → Notifications** to configure:

- Email notifications
- In-app alerts
- Digest frequency

### 5. Connect Your Accounts

Link external accounts to enrich your profile. See [Connections](/getting-started/connections) for details.

## Privacy Settings

Control who can see your profile information. Visit [Privacy & Visibility](/platform-fundamentals/privacy-and-visibility) to learn more.
