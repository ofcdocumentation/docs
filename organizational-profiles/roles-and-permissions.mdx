Roles and permissions determine what members of your organization can see and do on the platform. Proper role management keeps your organization secure while enabling effective collaboration.

## Organization Roles

Every member of your organization is assigned a role. Here’s an overview of the default roles:

| Role | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full access to all settings, content, and member management. Can transfer ownership. |
| **Admin** | Can manage members, settings, projects, and content. Cannot transfer ownership. |
| **Editor** | Can create and edit content, publish resources, and manage projects. |
| **Contributor** | Can create content and contribute to projects but cannot publish without approval. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to organizational content. |

## Managing Roles

### Adding Members

1. Navigate to **Your Organization → Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the email address or username
4. Assign a role
5. Click **Send Invitation**

### Changing a Member’s Role

1. Go to **Your Organization → Members**
2. Find the member whose role you want to change
3. Click the role dropdown next to their name
4. Select the new role

<Warning>
  Only Owners and Admins can change member roles. Owners are the only ones who can assign or remove the Admin role.
</Warning>

### Removing a Member

1. Go to **Your Organization → Members**
2. Find the member you want to remove
3. Click the **⋯** menu next to their name
4. Select **Remove from Organization**

## Custom Roles

If the default roles don’t fit your needs, you can create custom roles with specific permission sets:

1. Navigate to **Settings → Roles**
2. Click **Create Custom Role**
3. Name the role and configure permissions
4. Save and assign to members as needed
