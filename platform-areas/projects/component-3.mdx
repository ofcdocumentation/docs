The activity feed is the communication hub for your project — where updates are shared, discussions happen, and the team stays aligned.

## The Activity Feed

Every project has an activity feed that shows:

- **Posts** – Updates and announcements from team members
- **File uploads** – Notifications when new files are added
- **Member changes** – When people join or leave the project
- **Milestone updates** – Progress notifications
- **Comments** – Responses to posts and updates

## Creating Posts

### Write a Post

1. Navigate to **Project → Activity**
2. Click **New Post** in the composer at the top
3. Write your update
4. Optionally attach files or images
5. Choose who can see the post (all members or specific members)
6. Click **Post**

### Post Types

- **Update** – General project news and progress
- **Announcement** – Important notices that get highlighted
- **Question** – Prompts a structured discussion thread
- **Decision** – Records a decision made by the team

## Discussions

Click the **comment** icon on any post to start a discussion. Discussions support:

- **Threaded replies** – Reply to specific comments
- **Mentions** – Use `@username` to notify a team member
- **Reactions** – Quick responses without a full reply
- **Rich text** – Bold, italics, lists, and links

## Notifications

### What Triggers Notifications

You’ll receive notifications when:

- Someone posts in a project you’re a member of
- Someone comments on your post or a post you’ve commented on
- You’re mentioned in a post or comment
- A new file is uploaded

### Managing Your Notification Settings

Too many notifications? Customize them:

1. Go to **Project → Settings → Notifications**
2. Choose notification level:
   - **All activity** – Every post and comment
   - **Mentions only** – Only when you’re @mentioned
   - **Announcements only** – Only important posts
   - **None** – Turn off all project notifications

## Pinned Posts

Admins and Editors can pin important posts to the top of the activity feed:

1. Click **⋯** on the post you want to pin
2. Select **Pin to Top**

Pinned posts stay at the top until unpinned.

## Searching the Activity Feed

Use the search bar within the activity feed to find specific posts or discussions by keyword.
