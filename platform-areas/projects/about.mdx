Projects are the primary unit of collaborative work on the platform. They provide a dedicated workspace for teams to plan, coordinate, and deliver on shared goals.

## What Are Projects?

A project is a focused, collaborative workspace centered around a specific initiative, program, or goal. Each project has its own:

- **Member roster** – The people working on the project
- **File library** – Shared documents and assets
- **Activity feed** – Updates, discussions, and announcements
- **Impact Dashboard** – Data and metrics relevant to the project
- **Settings** – Visibility, permissions, and configuration

## Types of Projects

### Open Projects

Anyone on the platform can join and contribute. Great for community initiatives and open collaboration.

### Closed Projects

Membership by invitation or approval only. Best for teams that need controlled access.

### Private Projects

Visible only to members. Ideal for internal work or sensitive initiatives.

## Project Lifecycle

Projects move through stages:

1. **Planning** – Defining goals, team, and approach
2. **Active** – Work is underway
3. **Paused** – Temporarily on hold
4. **Completed** – Goals achieved, project archived
5. **Archived** – Preserved for reference
