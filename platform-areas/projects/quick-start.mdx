This guide walks you through creating and setting up a project from scratch.

## Step 1: Create a New Project

1. Navigate to **Platform Areas → Projects**
2. Click **\+ New Project**
3. Fill in the basic information:
   - **Project Name** – A clear, descriptive title
   - **Description** – What the project is about and its goals
   - **Category** – The type of work (optional)
4. Click **Create Project**

## Step 2: Configure Project Settings

After creation, configure your project’s settings:

### Visibility

Set who can see and join your project:

- **Public** – Anyone can view and request to join
- **Platform Members** – Only logged-in users can view
- **Closed** – Visible but requires approval to join
- **Private** – Only members can see the project

### Project Details

- **Cover Image** – A visual for your project page
- **Tags** – Keywords to help people find your project
- **Start and End Dates** – Optional timeline
- **Location** – Where the project is based or operates

### Affiliated Organization

Link your project to an organization or group (if applicable).

## Step 3: Invite Your Team

1. Go to **Project → Members**
2. Click **\+ Invite Members**
3. Search for people by name or email
4. Assign roles (see [Component #1](/platform-areas/projects/component-1) for role details)
5. Send invitations

## Step 4: Add Initial Files

Upload foundational documents to give team members context:

1. Go to **Project → Files**
2. Click **Upload** and select your files
3. Add descriptions to help teammates understand each file

## Step 5: Post a Welcome Update

Start the conversation with an introductory post on the activity feed:

1. Go to **Project → Activity**
2. Click **New Post**
3. Write a brief introduction for new members
4. Click **Post**

## Step 6: Set Up Your Impact Dashboard

Track your project’s progress with an Impact Dashboard:

1. Go to **Project → Impact**
2. Click **Create Dashboard**
3. Follow the [Impact Dashboard Quick Start Guide](/platform-areas/impact-dashboards/quick-start)

## You’re Ready!

Your project is live. Share the project link with potential collaborators or make it discoverable through the Projects directory.
