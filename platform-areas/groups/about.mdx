Groups are the community layer of the platform. They bring together people, organizations, projects, and resources around a shared mission, topic, or affiliation.

## What Are Groups?

A group is a community space that can:

- **Connect people** with shared interests or affiliations
- **Host projects** – Sponsor and coordinate collaborative work
- **Publish resources** – Share knowledge with the community
- **Facilitate discussion** – Keep members informed and engaged
- **Represent organizations** – Be the public face of a coalition or network

## Types of Groups

### Open Groups

Anyone can join instantly. Best for broad communities and public-facing networks.

### Closed Groups

Membership requires approval from an admin. Best for professional networks, coalitions, and communities with specific membership criteria.

### Secret Groups

Not visible in the public directory. Members must be invited. Best for internal teams or sensitive communities.

## Groups vs. Organizations

| Feature | Group | Organization |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Public profile | ✓ | ✓ |
| Host projects | ✓ | ✓ |
| Publish resources | ✓ | ✓ |
| Member directory | ✓ | ✓ |
| Billing/subscription | ✗ | ✓ |
| Platform-wide settings | ✗ | ✓ |

Groups exist within organizations — an organization can have many groups.
