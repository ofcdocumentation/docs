Effective group governance ensures your community runs smoothly, stays true to its mission, and grows sustainably.

## Group Roles

| Role | Capabilities |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full group control, including deletion and ownership transfer |
| **Admin** | Manage members, settings, content, and projects |
| **Moderator** | Moderate discussion, approve members, manage content |
| **Member** | Participate in discussions, access group content |
| **Guest** | View-only access to shared content |

## Managing Membership

### Approving Join Requests

For closed groups, manage incoming requests:

1. Go to **Group → Members → Pending**
2. Review each request (profile and optional message)
3. Click **Approve** or **Decline**
4. Approved members receive a notification

### Inviting Members

1. Go to **Group → Members**
2. Click **\+ Invite**
3. Search by name or email
4. Assign a role
5. Send the invitation

### Bulk Invitations

To invite many people at once:

1. Go to **Group → Members → Bulk Invite**
2. Upload a CSV with email addresses and roles
3. Preview and confirm

### Removing Members

1. Go to **Group → Members**
2. Click **⋯** next to the member
3. Select **Remove from Group**

## Membership Tiers

You can create custom membership tiers for groups with different levels of access or involvement:

1. Go to **Group → Settings → Membership Tiers**
2. Click **\+ Create Tier**
3. Name the tier and configure access level
4. Assign members to tiers as needed

## Group Governance

### Setting Group Norms

Add community guidelines visible to all members:

1. Go to **Group → Settings → Guidelines**
2. Write your community guidelines
3. Choose whether new members must agree to guidelines when joining

### Content Moderation

Moderators and Admins can:

- **Hide** posts or comments that violate guidelines
- **Remove** content from the group feed
- **Warn** members for guideline violations
- **Suspend** members temporarily

### Activity Audit Log

Admins can view a log of all administrative actions (member changes, content removals, setting changes) at **Group → Settings → Activity Log**.
