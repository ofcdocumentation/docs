This guide walks you through creating and configuring a group from scratch.

## Step 1: Create the Group

1. Navigate to **Platform Areas → Groups**
2. Click **\+ Create Group**
3. Fill in the basics:
   - **Group Name** – How the group will appear on the platform
   - **Description** – What the group is about and who should join
   - **Group Type** – Open, Closed, or Secret
4. Click **Create Group**

## Step 2: Complete the Group Profile

Make your group discoverable with a complete profile:

1. Click **Edit Profile**
2. Add:
   - **Cover Image** – A banner for the group’s page
   - **Logo** – A smaller icon or logo
   - **Website** – Link to an external site (optional)
   - **Focus Areas** – Topics this group covers
   - **Location** – If the group is geographically focused
3. Save your changes

## Step 3: Set Up Membership

Configure who can join and how:

1. Go to **Group → Settings → Membership**
2. Choose:
   - **Join Type**: Open (anyone), Approval Required, or Invitation Only
   - **Membership Questions**: Add screening questions for approval-based groups
   - **Default Role**: What role new members get when they join

## Step 4: Invite Founding Members

1. Go to **Group → Members**
2. Click **\+ Invite Members**
3. Search by name or email
4. Assign roles
5. Send invitations

<Tip>
  Start with a small founding group before opening membership broadly. Getting a few active members first builds momentum and culture.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Create Your First Post

Welcome members with an introductory post:

1. Go to **Group → Activity**
2. Click **New Post**
3. Introduce the group, its purpose, and what members can expect
4. Click **Post**

## Step 6: Add Initial Resources

Give members something valuable right away:

1. Go to **Group → Resources**
2. Publish or share existing resources that are relevant to the group

## Step 7: Launch a Group Project (Optional)

If your group has a specific initiative underway:

1. Go to **Group → Projects**
2. Click **\+ Create Project** or link an existing project
3. Follow the [Project Quick Start Guide](/platform-areas/projects/quick-start)
