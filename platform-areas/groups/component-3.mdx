Strong communication is the heartbeat of an active group. The platform provides several tools to keep your community informed and engaged.

## The Group Feed

The group feed is where all activity surfaces — posts, resource publications, project updates, and member announcements.

### What Appears in the Feed

- Posts from members
- Announcements from admins
- New resource publications
- Project milestones and updates
- New member welcome messages
- Event announcements

## Posting to the Group

### Creating a Post

1. Go to **Group → Activity**
2. Click **New Post**
3. Write your update, announcement, or question
4. Attach files, images, or links as needed
5. Set audience (all members or a subset)
6. Click **Post**

### Post Types

- **Update** – News and information for the community
- **Announcement** – Important messages that get pinned and highlighted
- **Discussion** – Opens a threaded conversation
- **Question** – Invites answers from the community
- **Event** – Notifies members of upcoming events

## Announcements

Announcements are high-priority messages that:

- Appear highlighted at the top of the feed
- Trigger email notifications to all members (or those who have enabled them)
- Can be pinned for an extended period

Only Admins and Moderators can post announcements.

## Discussion Threads

Grouped conversations allow deep engagement:

- **Reply** to any post to start a thread
- **Mention** members with `@username` to notify them
- **React** with emoji to signal agreement or support
- **Follow** a thread to receive notifications about new replies

## Member Notifications

### Group-Level Settings

Admins can set a default notification level for the group:

1. Go to **Group → Settings → Notifications**
2. Choose default: All activity, Announcements only, or None

### Individual Preferences

Each member can override the group default in their personal notification settings.

## Direct Messaging

Members can message each other directly using the platform’s messaging feature. Group membership doesn’t automatically grant messaging access — standard connection and privacy settings apply.

## Events

Post events to notify your group of upcoming meetings, webinars, or gatherings:

1. Click **New Post → Event**
2. Add event details (name, date, time, location or link)
3. Post to the group

Members can RSVP directly from the event post.
