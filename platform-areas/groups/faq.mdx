Answers to common questions about Groups on the platform.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can create a group?">
    Any platform member can create a group. To create a group on behalf of an organization, you’ll need Admin access to that organization.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how many groups I can create or join?">
    Free accounts can be active in up to 20 groups and create up to 5. Pro and Organization accounts have no limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can a group exist without being affiliated with an organization?">
    Yes. Groups can be independent communities created by any platform member, or they can be affiliated with an organization.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between a group and a project?">
    A group is a persistent community of people. A project is a focused, time-limited collaborative effort. Groups often host multiple projects over time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Membership

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I join a closed group without an invitation?">
    Yes — you can request to join a closed group. An admin will review your request and approve or decline it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see who's in a group before joining?">
    For open and closed groups, the member list is visible on the group’s public profile page. Secret group membership lists are not publicly visible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I leave a group?">
    Yes. Go to the group page and click **Leave Group**. Your past contributions (posts, files) will remain in the group.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can a group have subgroups?">
    Not directly, but groups can be affiliated with each other. You can create a network of related groups and link them in their descriptions or through the organization settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content and Activity

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can post in a group?">
    By default, all members can post. Admins can restrict posting to specific roles in **Group → Settings → Content Permissions**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see old posts and activity from before I joined?">
    For open and closed groups, new members can see historical activity. Secret groups may limit visibility based on admin settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can the group have its own email newsletter?">
    Not as a built-in feature, but Admins can send announcements that are delivered via email to members who have opted in to email notifications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deleting a Group

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete a group?">
    Only the group Owner can delete a group. Go to **Group → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Group**. This action is irreversible and removes all group content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to affiliated projects when a group is deleted?">
    Affiliated projects are not deleted with the group — they remain active but lose the group affiliation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
