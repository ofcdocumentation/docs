Resource Publishing lets you share your knowledge, research, and tools with the broader platform community. Published resources are discoverable by everyone on the platform and can be made publicly accessible.

## What Are Resources?

Resources are standalone pieces of content that your organization, project, or group wants to share broadly. They can be:

- **Reports and research** – Findings, studies, and analysis
- **Guides and how-tos** – Practical instructional content
- **Tools and templates** – Reusable assets others can adopt
- **Data and datasets** – Raw or processed data for the community
- **Multimedia** – Videos, podcasts, and interactive content
- **Funding opportunities** – Grants, fellowships, and other funding sources

## Why Publish Resources?

- **Build credibility** by showcasing your expertise and work
- **Support the community** with knowledge they can use
- **Increase visibility** – resources are featured in the platform directory
- **Enable collaboration** – others can contact you based on your resources

## Resource vs. Project File

| Feature | Project File | Published Resource |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Audience | Project members | Platform-wide |
| Discovery | Within project only | Searchable directory |
| Purpose | Internal collaboration | External sharing |
| Metadata | Basic | Rich (tags, license, etc.) |
