Answers to common questions about publishing resources on the platform.

## Publishing Basics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can publish resources?">
    Any platform member can publish resources. To publish on behalf of an organization or group, you’ll need Editor access or higher.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how many resources I can publish?">
    Free accounts can have up to 10 published resources. Pro and Organization accounts have no limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take for a resource to appear in the directory?">
    Resources typically appear in the directory within a few minutes of publishing. If your resource requires editorial review (for featured placement), that may take 2-3 business days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I co-publish a resource with another organization?">
    Yes. Add multiple organizations as co-publishers when filling in the attribution fields. Both organizations’ names will appear on the resource.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What types of content are allowed?">
    Resources should be substantive, relevant to the platform’s community, and accurate. Content that is spam, promotional without educational value, or violates our community guidelines will be removed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I publish content that was originally published elsewhere?">
    Yes, as long as you have the rights to share it. Use the external link option to point to the original source, or upload the file and note the original publication in the description.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What should I do if I find a resource that violates community guidelines?">
    Click **Report Resource** on the resource page. Our moderation team will review it within 48 hours.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updates and Versions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I update a resource after it's published?">
    Yes. You can edit the title, description, tags, and other metadata at any time. For file-based resources, you can upload a new version.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will people who downloaded the old version get notified of an update?">
    Yes, if they’ve saved the resource, they’ll receive a notification when a new version is published.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many versions can I keep?">
    The platform keeps the last 10 versions of a resource. Older versions are automatically archived after that.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Analytics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How far back does analytics data go?">
    Analytics data is available from the date the resource was published.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are analytics available for free accounts?">
    Basic analytics (views and downloads) are available to all users. Advanced audience analytics (demographics, referral sources) are available on Pro and Organization plans.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
