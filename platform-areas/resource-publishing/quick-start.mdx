This guide walks you through publishing your first resource on the platform.

## Before You Begin

- You’ll need an account with Editor or higher access within an organization, group, or project
- Have your content ready to upload (document, file, or link)

## Step 1: Start a New Resource

1. Navigate to **Platform Areas → Resources**
2. Click **\+ Publish Resource**
3. Choose how you’re adding content:
   - **Upload a file** – PDF, document, spreadsheet, etc.
   - **Add a link** – Link to external content
   - **Write content** – Create content directly on the platform

## Step 2: Add Metadata

Good metadata makes your resource easier to find. Fill in:

- **Title** – Clear and descriptive (aim for searchability)
- **Short Description** – A 1-2 sentence summary shown in search results
- **Full Description** – Detailed context for what the resource contains and how to use it
- **Tags** – Keywords that describe your resource’s topic and type
- **Resource Type** – Report, tool, dataset, guide, etc.
- **Publication Date** – When the content was created or is relevant to
- **Language** – Primary language of the resource

## Step 3: Set Attribution and License

- **Author(s)** – Who created this resource
- **Organization** – Which organization is publishing it
- **License** – How others may use the resource (e.g., Creative Commons)

<Tip>
  Using an open license like Creative Commons makes your resource more useful to others and signals that you’re committed to knowledge sharing.
</Tip>

## Step 4: Configure Visibility

- **Public** – Discoverable and accessible by anyone
- **Platform Members** – Visible to logged-in users only
- **Connected** – Visible only to your connections and group members

## Step 5: Preview and Publish

1. Click **Preview** to see how your resource will appear in the directory
2. Review all details for accuracy
3. Click **Publish**

Your resource will appear in the directory within a few minutes.

## After Publishing

- Share the resource URL with your network
- Track views and downloads from the resource’s analytics page
- Respond to comments and questions from the community
- Update the resource as new versions become available
