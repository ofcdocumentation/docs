Impact Dashboards give you the tools to measure, track, and communicate the outcomes of your work. Whether you’re reporting to funders, sharing progress with your community, or making strategic decisions, Impact Dashboards provide the visibility you need.

## What Are Impact Dashboards?

Impact Dashboards are customizable data visualization tools built directly into the platform. They connect to your projects, resources, and group activities to automatically pull in relevant data — or you can enter data manually.

## Key Features

- **Real-time data visualization** – Charts, graphs, and maps that update as your data changes
- **Custom metrics** – Define the indicators that matter most to your work
- **Multiple dashboard views** – Create different dashboards for different audiences
- **Data export** – Download your data in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats
- **Embeddable widgets** – Share dashboard components on external websites
- **Collaborative editing** – Multiple team members can contribute to dashboards

## Who Uses Impact Dashboards?

Impact Dashboards are built for:

- **Organizations** tracking program outcomes and reporting to funders
- **Projects** monitoring progress toward goals
- **Groups** communicating collective impact to the community
- **Researchers** tracking data across multiple initiatives
