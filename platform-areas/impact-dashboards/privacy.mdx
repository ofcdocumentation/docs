To add privacy settings to a Dashboard:

- Click on the gear icon at the bottom of the edit menu, this will open the privacy settings.
- Under “Select Privacy”, select an option, and click “Add”

<Info>
  **Privacy Types for Dashboards**

  -  **Public** – Allows the dashboard to be shared a link to view the dashboard, even if they are not logged into the platform
  - **Private** – Prevents the dashboard from being shared publicly. Visibility is restricted only to you or your organization and the projects you share it to. 
  - **Connections** – The content is visible to all of your direct connections
  - **Custom** – This allows for specific sharing rules, such as sharing with:
    - All groups
    - Specific groups
    - Specific individuals
    - With “allow” or “not allow” rules
</Info>

- To remove a privacy setting, click on the trash icon next to the list item you’d like to delete.
- Click “Update Page” to save changes

## Publishing and Sharing Dashboards

**To publish the dashboard to a project**:

- Go to the project you want to connect dashboard and navigate to the “Dashboard” tab
- Select the page you created in dropdown (next to edit selected page)
- Click “edit selected page” which will open the dashboard in a new tab and click “Update Page”
- Return to the dashboard tab to view the updated dashboard page. You may need to refresh the page

**Share a Dashboard URL**

If the dashboard is set to public, you can share the page through a URL.

To set the dashboard to public:

- Navigate to the edit the dashboard (To do this, select your icon in the top right, then “pages”, then select “Edit” under the gear next to the dashboard you want to work with)
- Click on the gear icon at the bottom of the edit menu, this will open the privacy settings.
- Under “Select Privacy”, click “Public”, then “Add”
- Click “Update Page”
